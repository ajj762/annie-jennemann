Welcome

Hello! I am a first year graduate student at the MU School of Journalism emphasizing in data journalism. I am passionate about analyzing data, building data visualizations and innovative methods of storytelling.

I currently work as a graphics editor at the Columbia Missourian. I teach students in the information graphics class how to clean data and create graphics and edit the graphics for publication.

Outside of the world of data, I love spending time with my two cats, Bean and Dany, and riding my bike.

You can reach me at ajennemann@mail.missouri.edu for questions about any of my work.
