Welcome!

Hello! I am a first year graduate student at the University of Missouri School of Journalism emphasizing in data journalism. I am passionate about analyzing data and designing graphics.

I currently work as a graphics editor at the Columbia Missourian for my graduate teaching assistantship. I teach students in the information graphics class how to clean data, create graphics using Adobe Illustrator, Tableau and Datawrapper and edit the graphics for publication.

I have a lot of experience working with Adobe Illustrator designing static graphics as well as Tableau, Datawrapper and Flourish Studio. I also have experience with HTML & CSS. I'm currently learning Javascript and D3.js. I also know DB Browser for SQLite and R for data analysis.

Outside of the world of graphics, I love spending time with my two cats, Bean and Dany, and riding my bike. And I don't mean to brag, but I'm pretty good at Catan.
